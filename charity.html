About Charity

MIXUE has partnered with Food Bank Malaysia to support families in need. For every artwork submitted, RM10 will go towards supporting the foods and school supplies.

Our mission is to end hunger and food waste in Malaysia. We believe that everyone deserves access to safe, nutritious food, and we are committed to making that a reality.
-Food Bank Malaysia, 2023-

Food Bank Malaysia is registered with The Registry of Societies of Malaysia as a non-profit that rescues edible surplus food and delivers it to those in need. From manufacturers to families, they bridge the gap between waste and nourishment, empowering communities and reducing food insecurity.

How Your Donations Help

Your contributions of RM10 or more will support providing food, school supplies, and educational classes for students in need.

For Charity

Foods All the raw, wet and dry ingredients will be used to cook meals and give to the families in need, ensuring they have nutritious food to sustain their daily lives. These meals aim to provide temporary relief to those who are struggling to afford daily necessities. Each meal is prepared with love and care to ensure that no one has to go hungry.
School Supplies All the school supplies will be distributed to the children to support their educational needs, ensuring they have the tools needed to excel in learning. These supplies aim to reduce the financial burden on their families and create a positive learning environment, inspiring them to reach their full potential.
